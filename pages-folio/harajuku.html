Harajuku Taphouse

Previous Next

For this redesign challenge, I chose Harajuku Taproom in Culver City, California. My goal was to improve the organization of information and to better represent the mood and style of the restaurant. I also wanted to highlight the beer selection as well as educate the user about izakaya style restaurants. For the purposes of the class, I changed the name to Harajuku Taphouse. My process included:

  • Research and Competitive Analysis
  • Site Map
  • Style Tiles
  • Wireframing
  • Visual Design
  • Responsive Prototype
