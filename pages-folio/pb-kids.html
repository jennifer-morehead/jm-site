These graphic art prints were created for two separate online art contests hosted by Minted.com and partnered with Pottery Barn Kids. Chosen from thousands of entries, Future Superhero and Stacked Plane were selected in 2016 and Windchimes, featuring gold foil printing, in 2017. Staying true to my minimalist aesthetic, my goal was to create contemporary art that would appeal to both kids and parents as well as compliment the Pottery Barn Brand.
