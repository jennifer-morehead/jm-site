My Tribe

Previous Next

As a parent, the struggle of managing and sharing information regarding my son with my family is persistent. My goal was to explore better solutions than the current organizational tools with parents and families in mind. From my research I learned that there were very few existing options outside of shared calendars. I found that having an easy way to share kids schedules, grocery lists and other information, securely with your family could alleviate some of the stress that comes with parenting. My solution was a privately shared mobile platform for families called My Tribe. My process included:

  • Research and Competitive Analysis
  • Persona Development
  • Wireframing
  • Visual Design
  • Prototype
